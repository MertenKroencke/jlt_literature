# Lay Literary Theory and Its Investigation through Digital Social Reading

Repository for the article *“Lay Literary Theory and Its Investigation through Digital Social Reading”* (JLT).

## Overview

The Jupyter Notebook `code/adjectives.ipynb` extracts adjectives modifying the word *“Literatur”* and compares them with adjectives modifying *“Buch”* and *“Text”* in a corpus of 2.86 million reviews from the German-language social reading platform LovelyBooks (https://www.lovelybooks.de/).

## Data

Due to copyright restrictions, the full review texts cannot be made publicly available.

However, a subset of the corpus is available via Zenodo:
https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17877135

In addition, the extracted adjectives together with their corresponding review URLs are provided in the `resources/` directory.
